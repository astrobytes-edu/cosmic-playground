My Recommendations

#14b8a6 (teal-500) - Best balance of visibility and reduced brightness. Still clearly teal, but won't "glow" as intensely. This is the most common choice when teal-400 feels too bright.

#20a89a (Deep Teal) - If you want even more subdued, this has richer depth while maintaining the aurora/cosmic feel. Good for a more premium/sophisticated look.
