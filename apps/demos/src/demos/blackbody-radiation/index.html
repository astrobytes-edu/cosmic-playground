What to notice 3 bullets
  • As temperature increases, the peak shifts to shorter wavelengths.
  • Total emission grows very rapidly with temperature ($\\propto T^4$).
  • “Red hot” vs “blue hot” depends on spectrum, not paint colors.
Model notes CGS + relative plot
  • The curve is generated from Planck’s law and plotted in relative (normalized) intensity.
  • Units: $\\lambda$ in cm internally (displayed in nm), $T$ in K.
  • The “star color” preview is a perceptual approximation (not full colorimetry) and is used only as a qualitative guide.
