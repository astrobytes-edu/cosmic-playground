What to notice 3 prompts
  • Increasing mass usually pushes stars toward hotter and more luminous parts of the ZAMS track.
  • Changing metallicity shifts $L$, $R$, and $T_{\rm eff}$ even at fixed mass.
  • Override presets are intentionally flagged because they are not ZAMS states.
Model notes Tout 1996 + units
  • ZAMS relations use Tout et al. (1996) analytic fits for $L(M,Z)$ and $R(M,Z)$.
  • Validity range: $0.1\le M/M_{\odot}\le100$ and $10^{-4}\le Z\le0.03$.
  • Effective temperature closure: $T_{\rm eff}=T_{\odot}\left[(L/L_{\odot})/(R/R_{\odot})^2\right]^{1/4}$.
