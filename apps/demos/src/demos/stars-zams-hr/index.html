    Step Guide 3 prompts
    1. Identify the main sequence, giant branch, and white dwarf region from the synthetic population map.
    2. Pick one hot-faint star and infer its radius using Stefan-Boltzmann scaling.
    3. Reveal mass colors and infer the mass-gradient direction along the main sequence.
    Model Notes Tout + CMD calibration
    • Main sequence properties use Tout et al. (1996) ZAMS fits for L(M,Z) and R(M,Z), with Teff from Stefan-Boltzmann closure.
    • Observer CMD mapping uses empirical bolometric correction and Teff-color calibration, plus deterministic Gaussian photometric scatter.
    • Radius guides follow L/L_sun = (R/R_sun)^2 (T/T_sun)^4.
