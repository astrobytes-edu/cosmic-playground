## MW Quick (4-6 min)

**Goal:** establish "discrete lines = discrete energy differences."

1. Set H-alpha ($n=3\to2$), ask students to predict visible/UV/IR.
2. Record $\lambda$ and $E_\gamma$.
3. Step to H-beta ($4\to2$), ask which quantity changes more and why.
4. Debrief with $E_\gamma = hc/\lambda$.

## MW Short (8-12 min)

**Goal:** series structure + convergence.

1. Keep Balmer filter active.
2. Students collect H-alpha, H-beta, H-gamma, H-delta.
3. Prompt: "What happens to spacing as $n_{\rm upper}$ increases?"
4. Connect trend to series limit language.

## Friday Lab (20-30 min)

**Goal:** Observable -> Model -> Inference.

1. Team A: emission run; Team B: absorption run.
2. Each team gathers a 4-row table (transition, $\lambda$, $E_\gamma$, series).
3. Swap tables and verify whether the same transitions appear.
4. Move to Elements tab and attempt one fingerprint match using pattern evidence.
